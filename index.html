About Bracelets by Haley

Bracelets by Haley is a company that specializes in making beautiful handmade bracelets for anyone to enjoy. All bracelets made by our company are 100% handmade and customize able using our website. We are located in the heart of Montreal, Vieux Port inside a 200 year old building with a historic structure setting the atmosphere to our lovely in person shop since 1996. We also have in-person workshops as well as video tutorials to teach people how to make their own bracelets and find others who enjoy the same practice. Our goal is to share our love for making beautiful bracelets with as many people as we can from all different walks of life, bracelet making is for everyone.

At Bracelets by Haley; our highly qualified staff is determined to making your experience with us as fabulous as can be and satisfying all your needs. We offer flexible hours and affordable product prices. If you would like to get a second opinion listen to what some of our satisfied customers have to say. If you have any further questions about our services, please fill out our inquiriesform. We also have links to other websites of similar interest.
